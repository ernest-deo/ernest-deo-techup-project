What is HoopHubSG?

HoopHubSG is a website designed to help fantasy basketball players make informed decisions and gain a competitive edge in their leagues by providing simple data visualization and analysis tools.

Erm I don't even play fantasy basketball, so why should I care?

That's fine - but if you have an interest in sports, statistics and the thrill of beating your league mates week-on-week, maybe give it a shot!

Ok...but how does HoopHubSG help me win?

  • Schedule Grid: Access the 2024-25 NBA schedule to look out for back-to-back games and teams with waiver wire pickups. This schedule has been adjusted for Yahoo Fantasy League players with seasons ending March 23 (recommended season end date).
  • Player Stats: Check out the player rankings across the top 200 players in fantasy over the 2024-25 season (rankings updated weekly).
  • Player Comparison: Compare players side-by-side to make optimal roster decisions.
  • Depth Charts: View depth charts for all 30 NBA teams to know the next-man-up when injuries hit.
  • Useful Links: Quick access to valuable NBA and fantasy basketball resources.
  • Upcoming Features: Live updates on player injuries, customisability to your fantasy team on Yahoo/ESPN/Fantrax.
