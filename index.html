What is HoopHub?

HoopHub is a website designed to help fantasy basketball players make informed decisions and gain a competitive edge in their leagues by providing simple data visualization and analysis tools.

Ok...but how does HoopHub help me win?

  • NBA Schedule: Access an interactive, color-coded NBA schedule to plan your matchups each week.
  • Player Stats: Updated weekly, dive deep into player ranks across each of the 30 NBA teams.
  • Player Comparison: Compare players side-by-side to make optimal roster decisions.
  • Useful Links: Quick access to valuable NBA and fantasy basketball resources.

Who designed this tacky looking website?

HoopHub is a project developed by one passionate basketball fan with 0 coding knowledge, access to AI LLMs, and a desire to WIN in his leagues. Any and all feedback is welcome, but please be gentle!
